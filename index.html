FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Are payouts instantaneous?

Monthly payouts from the trading program are automatically credited to your USDT account between the 1st and 3rd of each month.

Affiliate commissions are also paid monthly at the variable rate until the obligation is fulfilled.

SolanaPayouts operates through trading, ensuring that commissions are paid incrementally and sustainably.

How much can I earn?

We accept cryptocurrency as well as fiat money, wire transfers and credit cards through third-party payment providers.

How do I see the returns?

Monthly results are published on our website. Payments are made between the 1st and 3rd of the following month and are automatically credited to your USDT wallet.

Are returns guaranteed?

No, returns are not guaranteed.

This is a high-risk trading investment, and both profits and capital can be lost. We advise investing sensibly.

Can I compound my earnings?

While compounding is not available, you can open new positions with your paid-out profits at any time.

For example, if you deposit $10,000 and earn 20% in a month, you can re-deposit the $2,000 profit to potentially achieve a 5x return on that deposit.

Are you legit?

We are a legitimate Financial Services Company, Fully Licensed, Registered and Regulated by the Financial Analytical Office (FAÚ) in the Czech Republic, under Identification Number 19337779.

Our operations are transparent and credible and undergo regular audits for proof of funds deposited, project security and proof of trading.
